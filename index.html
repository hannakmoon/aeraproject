Project Introduction

What is Message Tree?

Message Tree is more than a website. It is a space for awareness, empathy, and participation. It begins by helping visitors understand the problem of unequal access to vaccines and healthcare, and then invites them to respond in a human way: by sending words of thanks to the people who continue to care for others in difficult conditions.

The tree symbolizes life, healing, growth, and hope. Each message becomes part of that growing tree, turning gratitude into something visible and shared.

Why it matters

Why start with awareness?

Before visitors can leave a message, they need to understand the issue. The next page explains why some communities still lack vaccines and healthcare access, and why doctors and nurses working in those places deserve recognition and support.
