Why it matters

Seeing the Gap Between Need and Care

This page compares two global stories: where people struggle because of limited healthcare access, and where doctors and nurses are sent to help.

Why compare two maps?

A single map can show where a problem exists. But two maps can show a deeper story. One map shows communities facing healthcare and vaccination barriers. The other map shows doctors and nurses working to support those regions.

This comparison helps us understand why gratitude matters. The Message Tree is not only about the problem. It is also about recognizing the people who keep showing up to care for others.
